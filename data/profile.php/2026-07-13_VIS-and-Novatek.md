# VIS and Novatek

VIS and Novatek

1. 因為AI和PMIC需求暢旺排擠產能，DDIC/TDDI相關的foundry (8"/12")高壓製程產能利用率高，1Q26 VIS、PSMC、SMIC開始漲價5~10%，2Q26上漲趨勢擴大，VIS擴大漲幅為10~15%，PSMC、SMIC繼續漲5%及5~10%，漲價廠商擴大到UMC漲5~10%，Nextchip(晶合)漲5~10%，HLMC漲5%

2. 雖然貼文談的是DDIC/TDDI相關產能，但不太可能只有漲這個製程，其他主流邏輯成熟製程應該也是同步上漲

3. 供應商原物料上漲的時候，有些產業要吸收漲幅，有些產業可以反映給客戶，有些產業還可以"加價"反映給客戶以提高自已的margin，端視"供應商議價力"和"同業競爭力"和"客戶議價力"(五力分析)三者誰強誰弱而定，DDIC產業，決定價格最大的因素通常是"同業競爭的強度"，因此當foundry漲價的時候，雖然成本上升，但因為沒有多餘產能來競爭殺價，同業競爭力度轉弱，尤其是常見的小型競爭者拿到wafer產能殺價搶市佔的情況將消失，DDIC業者往往能"加價"反應給客戶，也就是說foundry漲價、DDIC可漲價多一點，自己的margin往往能藉此提升，尤其對產能不餘匱乏的龍頭大廠較有利

4. NB DDIC+Tcon 2Q26漲價10~15%，Monitor DDIC 3Q26開始漲價，TV DDIC尚未，手機/平板低階TDDI也有漲價其中低階HD TDDI漲價最多2Q26漲15~30%， 2H26將繼續上漲

5. 雖然DDIC/TDDI大多屬於需求走弱的消費性電子產品，2H26需求不佳，但因為foudnry產能吃緊成本上漲，Omdia預料2H26 DDIC/TDDI將持續漲價

------------"隨著代工廠產能收緊及上游半導體製造成本上升，顯示驅動集成電路（DDIC）價格持續上漲。雖然2026年下半年的需求預計將低於上半年，但持續的成本上漲預計將支持進一步的DDIC價格上漲。
Display driver IC (DDIC) prices are rising as foundry capacity tightens and upstream semiconductor manufacturing costs increase. Although demand in the second half of 2026 is expected to be weaker than in the first half, continued cost increases are expected to support further DDIC price increases.

隨著電力管理積體電路（PMIC）需求快速成長，受AI相關應用推動，台灣代工廠正逐步減少用於電視、顯示器、筆記型電腦及觸控顯示驅動晶片（TDDI）的DDIC容量。
Taiwanese foundries are reducing available capacity for DDICs used in TVs, monitors, notebook PCs, and touch and display driver integration chips (TDDIs), as demand for power management integrated circuits (PMICs) continues to grow rapidly, driven by AI-related applications.

這一轉變正在多個應用領域收緊 DDIC 生產能力。同時，上游半導體製造所用的關鍵原料價格也呈現顯著上漲趨勢。包裝與製造中使用的關鍵金屬價格大幅上漲，包括黃金、白銀和銅，推高了晶圓鑄造、包裝及測試作業的整體成本。
This shift is tightening DDIC production capacity across several applications. At the same time, key raw materials used in upstream semiconductor manufacturing are also experiencing a significant upward price trend. Prices for critical metals used in packaging and manufacturing, including gold, silver, and copper, have risen sharply, increasing the overall costs of wafer foundry, packaging and testing operations.

因此，包括先鋒Vanguard International Semiconductor，VIS、Powerchip Semiconductor Manufacturing Corporation PSMC及SMIC在內的代工廠，於2026年第一季季度（QoQ）將8吋晶圓廠的晶圓價格（QoQ）調漲5-10%。PSMC也在2026年第一季調漲了HV90nm晶圓在12吋晶圓廠的晶圓價格。2026年第二季晶圓價格持續上漲，越來越多晶圓廠也跟著漲價趨勢。
As a result, foundries including Vanguard International Semiconductor (VIS), Powerchip Semiconductor Manufacturing Corporation (PSMC), and Semiconductor Manufacturing International Corporation (SMIC) increased wafer prices at 8-inch foundry fabs by 5-10% quarter-on-quarter (QoQ) in 1Q26. PSMC also increased its wafer prices for HV90nm at 12-inch foundry fabs in 1Q26. Wafer prices continued to rise in 2Q26, and more foundries joined the trend of raising prices.

不受地緣政治限制的大範圍DDIC訂單，正逐步從台灣的8吋晶圓廠轉移到中國大陸的12吋晶圓廠，導致大陸晶圓廠的使用率很高。矽片價格預計在2026年下半年持續上漲。
Large-area DDIC orders that are not subject to geopolitical restrictions are gradually shifting from Taiwan’s 8-inch fabs to mainland China’s 12-inch fabs, resulting in high utilization rates at mainland fabs. Wafer prices are likely to continue rising in 2H26.

晶圓製造佔DDIC成本的最大部分，佔總成本的60–70%。其中晶圓成本佔40%。這表示晶圓價格的任何調漲都會直接影響DDIC供應商的成本結構：
Wafer fabrication accounts for the largest portion of DDIC costs, comprising 60–70% of the total. Within this, wafer cost accounts for 40%. This means that any increase in wafer pricing has a direct impact on DDIC suppliers’ cost structure: 

-對設計廠而言，較高的晶圓成本是提高DDIC價格以反映生產成本上升的明確理由。For design houses, higher wafer costs provide a clear reason to raise DDIC prices in order to reflect rising production costs.

-筆記型電腦的 DDIC 已經開始感受到影響。由於市場由台灣設計廠主導，且筆記型電腦 DDIC 搭配時序控制器（T-con），筆記型電腦 DDIC 價格在 2026 年第二季上漲了 5-15%。Notebook PC DDICs are already seeing the impact. Since the market is dominated by Taiwanese design houses, and notebook PC DDICs are paired with timing controllers (T-cons), notebook PC DDIC prices increased by 5-15% in 2Q26.

-Monitor DDICs 可能在 2026 第三季出現價格上漲，而電視 DDIC 仍面臨定價挑戰。Monitor DDICs may see price increases in 3Q26, while TV DDICs continue to face pricing challenges.

-智慧型手機與平板TDDI價格也在2026年第二季上漲，主要因HV90nm製程節點產能減少。HD 智慧型手機 TDDI 價格在 2026 年第二季上漲了 15-30%，預計下半年仍將持續上漲。Smartphone and tablet TDDIs’ prices also increased in 2Q26, mainly due to reduced capacity at the HV90nm process node. HD smartphone TDDI prices increased by 15-30% in 2Q26 and are forecast to continue rising in 2H26.

Omdia資深分析師蔣與楊表示：「目前DDIC價格上漲並非由需求驅動，而是供給減少。」「雖然2026下半年的需求預計將低於2026年上半年，但持續的成本上漲仍將推高DDIC價格，以反映這些成本上升。」“The current wave of price increases for DDICs is not driven by demand, but rather by a reduction in supply,” said Queenie Jiang, Senior Analyst at Omdia. “Although demand in 2H26 is expected to be weaker than in 1H26, persistent cost increases will still drive up DDIC prices to reflect these rising costs.”
